About Campus Connect

Campus Connect is an intracollege social platform to help students connect by interests, collaborate on projects, join study groups, and discover on-campus events.

Project Details

  • Tech stack: React (frontend) / Node.js + Express (backend) / PostgreSQL (DB)
  • Features: Interest matching, groups, events, chat, admin moderation
  • Privacy: College-only access via verified emails; secure auth & data protection

Frequently asked

How to join groups?

Go to Groups → Explore → Click Join on a group. Private groups require invite or moderator approval.

How to report inappropriate content?

Open the post and click Report. Admins will review within 24 hours.
